# Task 2 — Permutations

---

## Definitions / Theory

Permutation:

$$
n!
$$

Special techniques:

| Method | Use |
|--------|----|
| Block | keep objects together |
| Complement | subtract unwanted cases |

---

## Solutions

---

### 1. 8 books

$$
8! = 40\,320
$$

---

### 2. Two together

| Step | Value |
|------|------|
| Block size | 7 objects |
| Internal order | 2 |

$$
7! \cdot 2 = 10\,080
$$

---

### 3. Not together

$$
8! - 7! \cdot 2 = 30\,240
$$

---

### 4. First fixed

$$
9! = 362\,880
$$

---

## Key Insight
